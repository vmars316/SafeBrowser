   Welcome to SafeBrowser Editor .
Herein is a listing of the TWO 'Approved Link Files' .
One contains   'Whole Sites Links' 'named: 'SakerPlats.swd' (in Swedish it means 'Safe Sites') .
The other contains   'Specific Links' 'named: 'SakerLank.swd' (in Swedish it means 'Safe Links')
'Whole Sites Links' means that the Whole Site is Approved , the User can go anywhere on that site .
'Specific Links' means that only certain Links on a Site are Approved .   For example , you may not want the User to have access to everything on YouTube , only certain videos ,
Both of these areas can be Edited to add/change/delete Links . If you Edit these areas be sure to Click on Save All Files to save your Changes .
